Why Ager ?

Ager is a co-operative based contract farming platform. It is aimed at solving the problem of lack of farmer visibility by creating a platform where farmers can be onboarded irrespective of their locations around Nigeria inorder to easily receive the necessary credit/input. According to the Food and Agricultural Organisation of the United Nations, Nigeria has 70.8 million hectares of agriculture land area with maize, cassava, guinea corn, yam beans, millet and rice being the major crops. Inspite of this, only 57 percent of the 6.7 million metric tons of these crops are consumed in Nigeria annually is locally produced leading to a deficit of about 3 million metric tons, which is either imported or smuggled into the country illegally.This problem has been partly attributed to a lack of adequate agricultural inputs in the right quality and quantity and lack of farmer visibility.

Our Model

field officer onboards a farmer onto the ager platform

farmer goes to form a trust group

farmer is given loans in the form of inputs(fertilizer, seed etc)

farmer applies inputs to his/her farm

field officer monitors farmer's fields

Farmer harvest his produce, and repays his loan with 20% of his harvest.
